All you need to know about your Business Revolve Credit Card

  • Overview

    • Targeted at the previously unacquired or less acquired market of the SME. This is typically customers who fall into the R0 to R5 million (small) and R5 million to R50 million (medium) annual turnover group. 
    • Most of our customers are start-ups and sole proprietors that require a different solution than your normal large merchants due to their set-up.

  • Benefits and features

    • Pay as little as 3% of the outstanding balance monthly
    • Up to 55 days interest-free facility if full statement balance is made
    • No transactional fees on all POS purchases
    • Free lost card protection
    • Elite Car Park – Visa Commercial Cardholders receive a preferential rate of R85 per day
    • Bidvest lounges – Visa Cardholders receive 15% discount on lounge access charges at all Bidvest Premier Lounges
    • Revolving credit facility
    • Travel insurance - Automatic Travel Insurance Cover is provided to travellers provided you purchase your air ticket using your Business Revolve Credit Card
    • Optional travel insurance can be purchased if you require additional cover
    • SMS or email notifications can be sent to alert the cardholder whenever a transaction is performed.

  • How it works

    • Entry-level credit card for small and medium enterprises (SMEs)
    • Credit limit approved at account level
    • If the main account holder decides to apply for a secondary card, the secondary card holder will have access to the same credit limit as the main account holder.

  • Value adds and Rewards

    • Business Revolve Cards can be used at ATMs, POS terminals and for online purchases 
    • The card can also be linked to Business Online Banking and can be registered for NotifyMe
    • The credit limit approved is on account level
    • If you decide as the main account holder to issue a secondary card to an employee, you and your employee will have the same credit limit 
    • Revolving credit facility and controlled everyday business spending
    • Consolidate business expenses
    • Solve your working capital needs
    • Cost-effective
    • One credit limit shared between multiple cards and can be increased/deceased as needed.
